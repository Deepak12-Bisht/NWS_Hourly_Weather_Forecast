Finding the Forecast Area Endpoint:¶

To find your weather forecast from the national weather services, you can not directly use your latitude and longitude.¶

To find the forecast area from your latitude,longitude.,the NWS API provide the /points/ endpoints that uses a latitude,longitude.¶

In [3]:
pointUrl="https://api.weather.gov/points/33.93549789914974,-84.3447666066148"
